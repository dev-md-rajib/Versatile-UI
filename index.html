Take a Challenge!

Take a challenge to complete in daily basis and make better version of yourself. It includes focusing on different parts of your body.

Push-Ups

3x15

Push-ups build upper body strength by working the chest, shoulders, and triceps, while also engaging the core for improved stability and overall functional fitness.

Bench Tricep Dips

3x15

Bench tricep dips primarily target the triceps, helping to strengthen while also engaging the shoulders and chest to enhance upper body stability and strength.

Front Raise

3x12

Front raises strengthen the front part of the shoulders, improving shoulder strength and posture, while also aiding in injury prevention by stabilizing the shoulder.

Side Jumps

3x12

Side jumps are a plyometric exercise that boosts cardiovascular endurance, strengthens the lower body (particularly the quads, hamstrings, and calves) and balance.
